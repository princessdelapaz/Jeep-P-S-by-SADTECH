What is Jeep-P-S?

The etymology of the name "Jeep-P-S" is a mixture of the terms "Jeepney" and GPS. The name of our website was created with the mainstream filipino transportation and the most widespread navigation system in mind. This is also because the website wants its users to be able to navigate Metro Manila through its transportation infrastructure and commuting networks. With this in mind, Jeep-P-S symbolizes the websites goal to allow its users to be able to travel around Manila safely, quickly, and cheaply through the use of various public transportation systems


Purpose

The purpose of Jeep-P-S is to help users find the best route in getting from point A to point B depending on their needs through community reviews. Users will be able to search for specific routes based on their destination or starting point and filter it to their preferences. It also aims to inform other users of other things, like the security of the selected route, possible events, constructions, and etc. In summary, the application aims to bring together a community that aims to interact and inform each other of vital commuting information in Metro Manila.

Features

Jeep-P-S has a forum as its main community interaction feature. The forum feature allows the users to interact with each other as explained in the overview of the system. The forum will consist of guides that enables the users to travel around Metro Manila with ease. The users will be able to discuss the different routes and to also find the shortest, quickest, cheapest, and most secure routes that the users can take. The users are also able to vote which route is best to take during time or day. The users of the forum can use these guides to use the posted routes or confirm the claims of each post.
