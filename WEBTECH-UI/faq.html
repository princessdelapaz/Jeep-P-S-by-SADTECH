What's an upvote?

An upvote means community members find the comment or guide really helpful to them. It can also mean that the post is a quality one.

What's the purpose of an upvote and a downvote?

A upvote mean that the post is realible and is trustworthy. A downvote is the opposite. The number of upvotes and downvotes relies entirely on the votes of the community. In the end, it's up to you to determine what you think works for you.
